ACM Club Wins National Tech Fest

The ACM Club represented our college in the Bhutan TechFest 2024 and won 1st prize in autonomous robot design. This achievement inspired many students to pursue innovation and technology.

Cultural Club Hosts Bhutanese Heritage Week

The Cultural Club organized a full week of performances, exhibitions, and traditional art workshops. Over 800 students participated, bringing together culture, history, and fun.

Coding Club Builds Mental Health App

In collaboration with the health center, the Coding Club launched a web app that provides mental wellness tips and schedules reminders. This was a huge step in promoting student well-being using technology.
